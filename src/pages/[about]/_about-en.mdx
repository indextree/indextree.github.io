# About Me

Hi, I'm **Nik Klemenc** — a UX/UI Designer and Front-End Developer based in Slovenia.

I'm passionate about creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and intuitive. My work sits at the intersection of design and code, where I get to think deeply about how people interact with technology and then build solutions that genuinely help them.

## What I Do

- **UX & UI Design** — I design user interfaces with a focus on clarity, usability, and aesthetics. From wireframes and prototypes to polished design systems, I cover the full design process.
- **Front-End Development** — I bring designs to life with clean, accessible, and performant code. I'm especially interested in modern web technologies like Astro, Svelte, and Tailwind CSS.
- **Design Systems** — I love building and maintaining design systems that help teams ship consistent, high-quality products faster.

## This Blog

This is my personal space on the web where I share thoughts on design, technology, and the ever-evolving digital landscape. Topics I write about include:

- User experience and interface design
- Front-end development techniques and tools
- Design systems and component-driven development
- AI and its impact on design and development workflows
- Reflections on remote work and digital culture
